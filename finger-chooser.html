Finger Chooser

Random selection made simple

About Finger Chooser

Finger Chooser is a random selection app that helps users make decisions by randomly picking fingers on a touchscreen.

Primary Use Cases

  • Deciding who goes first in a board game
  • Settling disputes about who buys the next round of drinks
  • Splitting people into groups

Key Features

The application offers three distinct operational modes:

Single Mode

Selects one finger from those displayed

Multi Mode

Picks 2-5 fingers simultaneously

Group Mode

Divides fingers into 2-5 separate groupings

Platform Limitations

Device hardware constrains functionality:

  • Android: Supports maximum 10 simultaneous fingers
  • iOS: Supports up to 5 fingers

Note: These represent device capabilities rather than app restrictions.

For scenarios exceeding device finger-tracking limits, Random Race provides alternative randomization functionality.
